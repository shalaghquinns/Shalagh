Our minds suck on the numbers and are satisfied by the plans. We always seek to make more and more sense of what we’re doing, who we are, an how much we can make of our time before it runs out. Our senses are driven towards all external factors attempting to add up and actualize our relationship with our world.

We lost our ability to tend to the inner crops of sense making, when we were handed a screen that could feed us any world we could imagine, without our need to find and structure the imagination within our mind. It is not the product of our creation, it is the person we create because we made the product.
