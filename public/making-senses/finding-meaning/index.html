Making Senses

we want our life to mean something we need a reason.

For a long time, the reason was survival. Now we are here surviving, doing much more than just surviving and we still need a reason. we can’t bare being helpless for it doesn’t make sense to just be here and not have hope

hope waits around each corner, hope has many streams of awareness inside like a dice, hope has many sides, something is bound to land like a dice, the hope is cut by corners and edges that slice it into a shape with sides we loose the whole picture the meaning is fragmented cut by the need for numbers,

numbers fragment the mind

books try and convince us sentence by sentence building on something that makes logical sense they bring us along in a story, we read and they tell us what to think our thoughts become the book, and we enjoy how its presented in a way that our mind can lay back on a merry go round, to be constantly fed information into our head.

We greatly enjoy the pleasure of being fed information. The sooth of the mind as it relaxes into a constant feed of what ever meets my need,

This comes from our need to feel a part of whats happening here, the system of liking and watching a stream is a symptom of this need,

The number enforces the story, and the story enforces the numbers and together we go round around around until the whole world is created and defined by me and all the power we’ve given make meaning is in the hands of this tiny phone game we’re are playing,

the only way to change the game, is to play the change

We are helpless to a system, sure delete your social media, or rebel against the way its tried to control us how its taken away our ability to make meaning from the world, notice how it stops you from sharing your truth to your friends, notice how it keeps us participating in a formula, notice how this participation hinders your creativity notice the box it has put you in and break free

participate to free yourself participate to engage in another reason to make
