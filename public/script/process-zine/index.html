the process zine is like a joke in that i never know where its going on, but it always has a punch line to give me, a funny way that makes everything it did before make sense

this project as a metaphor for trusting the creative process, I wanted to see what was meant to learn from this process and the project because the creative stream I would use to practise documenting and following this thing i trusted so deeply in, the process, and what happened was a deepening, each layer would open me into a vaster understanding, I began by eliminating any timeline I had on the project, then I had to eliminate the need to share the process, then it opened into many streams and sides I began breaking down the natural process of creation through the stars and creating my own language which taught me I had to find another way of understanding this experience and then I had to loose the need to understand it and wrap my head around it, and now I am playing through multiple streams different games on different layers of experiences in things we call instagram accounts and each one signifies a different layer of my conscious experience
