Social Media

the internet has a beautiful power, to connect us gives us endless possibility to interact with the people of our kin, our our truth in a new form.

Until now our connection to each other has been hindered by race, social group, and religon. Until now we have been connecting to people through a shared beleif systems and social structures from the world around us.

The internet has a power to connect us with people of the same truth, of the same inner beleif systems, of the same inner worlds, but the power of the beast, requires us to learn to tell a new truth, a new story to our brothers and sisters, one that is of an inner truth, a true truth. Not of the one the world around us has projected upon us, we are made to break the image this world has projected onto itself, because it is hurting our souls, we are at war, fighting our brothers and sisters, about these made up systems, about these external games, money, numbers, power, and religion.

We need to learn to speak of a new truth, the one we are really having, and we will find those who will support us. and we need to do it now, because the story we are telling to eachother has gotten out of hand,

share as your participation to the rebellion of that which is not working any more, share the stupid, share the fun, become embarrassed of your self, turn the image inside out, we have to dramatize it, play out our karma.
