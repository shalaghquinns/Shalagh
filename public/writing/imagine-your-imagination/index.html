remember its power when we were young and could get wrapped up for hours in our invented mind,

and somewhere along the lines we had to give over this power to what was real, what was proof, what was good, what was cool

we lost the ability to invent this place into our home

creativity is not an art practise it is not something we make time for it is never separate from any moment its always happening with each moment we make more and more sense of our world we are imagining into our reality

the experience is is racked with possibility, and we are suppose to choose a direction, we think about it logically, we add it up in our head, we come up with a reason based on what we know, and we form our path forward based on this reason,

we are kidding ourself, we do not know what we know, because where ever we go, what we know expands,

we want to keep track of this knowing, rationalize it, in the creation of the rational we are excluding an experience that begs to be heard,

nonsense creeps through the sentences we’re explaining to each other, its haunts our senses as we scroll through our phones attempting to grasp and integrate all these sides into our world. we want to stand on top, be the one who knows, but the situation is bursting at the seems with the feeling that it doesn’t add up and no one knows what they are doing here,

and this is the truth, no one on earth, has any idea how they ended up here. thats the only fact.

we think we know what it means but that would mean we know what we think, and thoughts aren’t for knowing, and thinking doesn’t find meaning,

we like to use our thoughts to add it up, but it doesn’t add up in the way we think

it adds up on other layers, in other dimensions and we shant waste what mental energy we have attempting to logically understand this world

this is a ocean for a creative process to unfold

it has many streams underneath
