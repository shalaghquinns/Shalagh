but where are the facts what is the evidence that can prove this. fact comes from science, science cannot fathom the existence of both fact and perception

we are both getting to know our world, and getting to know what it means truth is both fact and perception

both are based on each other, never separate co existing in a dance to get us to grasp and sense into our world

we love to search for facts and for our perception to understand its truth through facts, facts create structure

when we let the perception search past the facts a sense of truth and structure begins to arise that has its own sense of fact, infact.

Leaning to trust perception, craves the path of inner knowing, and allows us to rely less on external evidence

when we lean to far on the facts of the known world the inner perception does very little seeking to be satisfied. and when we do search for what we want to perceive, we turn to scrolling worlds that satisfy our perceptions desire without our perception actually having to imagine into it.
