Writing Dones

imagine a world without this screen…

in this world, anything you want to create you must find it in reality, you must engage in the steps forward to put what you imagination into action, we must work into the vision of what we want to see happen we have to make sense of the problem, to make sense from non sense

we have to create how we sense, to give life to the unknown, and along the way, the conversation between our senses stitches together the known and unknown, and the process becomes a dance, of revealing itself.

we feel at home here on earth in this dance, we feel supported, we feel connected, we feel a part of the experience,

art is creating our own sense of the world,

it doesn’t matter what it is it doesn’t matter what it looks like

its in the process of creating it, we are making up our senses, we are forging a path, based on our internal freedom about how we want to envision this life

by putting the vision into action, we become active participants in the sensory experience of the world

we come to touch our experience from the inside out

the intuitive experience stems from looking at how your internal world responds to its environment rather than how you are responding to your external world

we have so much beautiful art out there and amazing creations everything our senses crave to create can met by the screen

but this does not satisfy our creative expression and instead blocks us from being creativity engaged with the world because the screen world appears more interesting

we need to stop staying stuff on here designed to help other make sense of an external reality,

we want to make so much sense to someone that they make new sense of them self
so we try very hard to make sense to others and forget we are the only ones making sense of this place anyways

we are infinity tied into tiny human bodies, here to comprehend karma, so that the sun can have energy to shine
