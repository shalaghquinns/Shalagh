Times

we use time to define time, an idea we created has now created itself into us

everything we do is in reference to time our conscious experience is in a calculative dance with the time thats moving around us

we want to do the most with it, we want to make some prof of it, we want cash in our hands to know our time has value

and the numbers give us the prof of the value of the time, they give us meaning, they give us food on our table. through numbers we survive so it makes sense that we give them a lot of credit

but in many cases, we give the numbers priority over another voice choosing the known over the unknown sacrificing a part of ourself that we might know or might never know

time is running inside not on a line
