These differences create conflict.

We are all coming from different parts of the world, different belief systems and this creates a lot of problems. Yet it is a crime to recognize differences, because its like we are recognizing someone for where they came from. However, the difference does not exist between us on the outside, It comes from deep inside.

We are the world we are born into, the belief system of this space becomes deeply ingrained into our behaviour. These belief systems have developed over the years where they have grown into forms of power. Fights start as the beliefs systems take the power from the other belief systems, and each one try to gain their power back. These worlds have been flighting with as far back as it goes.

In the world I come from this is a myth, not a part of the real experience. We have no idea how to imagine that conflict would arise when different belief systems come together. Where I come from we witness a lot of harmony between our difference, everyone behvaves, there are no threats and we are protected.

I think this creates a lot of consequences within the western world. We are secluded from the effects of war and real problems in our daily lives. Obviously this sounds like a good thing, however it means at the root, we don’t know how to fathom real problems are happening, and we have not learned yet how to take responsibility for this real reality.

Turns our these different belief systems create conflict that are alive and ripe to eat. This place is helping me to develop a belief system for the individual experience. I hope it creates a lot of conflict with our inner worlds, I hope it helps to give rise to a new external world, through the process.
